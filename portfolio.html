Homelessness in America

Homelessness is rising across Tennessee, especially in cities like Nashville and Knoxville. With housing costs going up and resources stretched thin, more people and families are finding it harder to stay in stable housing. This Story Map takes a closer look at the growing crisis, regional shelter beds availability, and the biggest challenges keeping people from finding a permanent place to live.
